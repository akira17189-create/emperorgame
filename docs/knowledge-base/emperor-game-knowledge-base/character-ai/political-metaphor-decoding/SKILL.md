# Political Metaphor Decoding

## When to Use
- When analyzing imperial poems or calligraphy with hidden meanings
- When interpreting auspicious signs or omens (祥瑞)
- When decoding symbolic inscriptions or writings
- When understanding ambiguous court communications

## Core Workflow

### 1. Identify the Symbol or Metaphor
- Note the exact content (text, object, image)
- Identify the source (Emperor, official, discovered object)
- Determine the context and timing

### 2. Analyze the Symbolic Elements
- Break down the metaphor into components
- Identify what each element represents
- Consider historical and cultural associations
- Note any wordplay or double meanings

### 3. Connect to Political Context
- Identify current political situation
- Consider relevant persons and factions
- Assess recent events or tensions
- Understand the power dynamics

### 4. Decode the Hidden Meaning
- Determine the intended message
- Identify the target of the message
- Understand the implied action or response
- Assess the political implications

### 5. Determine Appropriate Response
- Decide whether to acknowledge the hidden meaning
- Plan the appropriate action or response
- Consider the risks of misinterpretation
- Execute the response strategically

## Key Considerations
- Imperial metaphors often carry multiple layers of meaning
- Misinterpretation can have severe consequences
- Context is crucial for accurate decoding
- Some metaphors are intentionally ambiguous
