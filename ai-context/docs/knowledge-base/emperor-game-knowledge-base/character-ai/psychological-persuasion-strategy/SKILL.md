# Psychological Persuasion Strategy

## When to Use
- When a subordinate or family member resists an imperial order
- When someone needs to accept an unpleasant separation or loss
- When emotional resistance blocks compliance with authority
- When immediate compliance is required despite emotional distress

## Core Workflow

### 1. Identify the Resistance
- Understand the emotional attachment
- Identify the specific objection
- Assess the intensity of resistance
- Determine the underlying need

### 2. Establish Authority Hierarchy
- Identify the highest authority (Emperor)
- Establish the chain of command
- Emphasize the authority's care for the resistant person
- Frame compliance as respecting the authority

### 3. Reframe the Situation
- Change the narrative from loss to temporary separation
- Emphasize positive outcomes of compliance
- Create a logical connection between compliance and desired result
- Use "exchange logic" (compliance now = faster return)

### 4. Enforce Compliance
- Use physical intervention if necessary
- Remove visual triggers of distress
- Give immediate, clear commands
- Follow through with promised actions

## Key Techniques
- **Authority Appeal**: "Who is the greatest? The Emperor"
- **Care Reframing**: "The Emperor cares about you most"
- **Exchange Logic**: "Let him leave fast, he returns fast"
- **Physical Intervention**: Block sight, provide comfort
- **Immediate Command**: Clear, direct orders
